Book the earliest possible slot — 10:00 is coolest and least crowded. The mosaic dragon staircase, serpentine bench, and gingerbread gatehouses are fantastic for kids. Allow ~2 hours. The free outer park area is also beautiful. Metro L3 to Lesseps or Vallcarca, then a short walk or bus 116.

12:00 — Passeig de Gràcia stroll & the Block of Discord
Take metro L3 from Lesseps to Passeig de Gràcia (15 min). Stroll Barcelona's most elegant boulevard — wide tree-lined pavements, Gaudí's snowflake-patterned tiles underfoot, ornate lamp posts, and the jaw-dropping Modernista buildings. Kids will notice how alien Gaudí's buildings look compared to everything else. Look up constantly.

Transport & tolls
Key tips
🎟️
Book everything in advance
Sagrada Família, Park Güell, and the Aquarium all sell out weeks ahead in June. Buy online — you save money and guarantee your slot. Same for PortAventura. El Pòsit in Cambrils also needs a reservation in summer.
🕑
Use the menú del día at lunch
Starter + main + dessert + drink for €12–16/adult at lunch. Have lighter, cheaper dinners. This is how you eat very well in Spain without overspending. Kids often get half-portions for much less.
🍎
Bring packed food to parks
Tibidabo, PortAventura, and Caribe all have very expensive food inside. Barcelona Zoo explicitly allows your own food with picnic areas. Buy a 6-pack of water from a supermarket (under €2) instead of buying individual bottles.
🚇
T-Casual metro card in Barcelona
€12.15 for 10 trips — reloadable, works for the whole family on each tap. Far cheaper than buying single tickets. Covers all metro lines including L9 from the airport.
⚠️
Tibidabo is priced by height, not age
Under 120cm = €14 · Over 120cm = €35 (adult price). Check your kids' heights before booking. Also confirm the park is open on 25 June on tibidabo.cat — Wed–Sun from 22 June.
🛣️
AP-7 motorway tolls
Barcelona ↔ Salou via AP-7 is ~€8–12 each way in tolls. Budget for 3 toll journeys total (El Prat → Tibidabo → Salou on 25 Jun, and Salou → El Prat on 1 Jul). Keep a card handy at the booth.
🌡️
Weather & packing
28–33°C throughout the trip. Pack factor 50 sunscreen (especially for the kids), hats, refillable water bottles, and light clothing. A small rucksack for park days is essential — packed lunch, water, sunscreen, change of clothes.
🚑
Emergency numbers in Spain
General emergency: 112 · Police (Mossos d'Esquadra): 088 · Medical emergency: 061 · All work from any phone including foreign numbers without needing a local SIM.
