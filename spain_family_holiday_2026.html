Money-saving tips
🎟️
Book everything online in advance
Sagrada Família, Park Güell, and the Aquarium all require advance booking in June. You save money vs. door prices and guarantee your slot. Same for PortAventura and Tibidabo.
🕐
Lunch is the main meal of the day
The menú del día at lunch (€12–16/adult) gives you starter + main + dessert + drink. Have a lighter dinner. This dramatically cuts daily food costs for the whole trip.
🍎
Bring snacks and picnic lunches to parks
Food inside theme parks (PortAventura, Caribe, Tibidabo) is very expensive. The Barcelona Zoo explicitly allows your own food with dedicated picnic areas. Buy water at a supermarket — a 6-pack costs under €2.
🚇
Metro in Barcelona, car in Salou
In Barcelona, use the T-Casual card (€12.15 for 10 metro trips). For Tibidabo, park at BSM Sant Genís (cheaper) and take TibiBus T2B up. In Salou, your rental car gives full flexibility.
⚠️
Tibidabo height-based pricing
Tickets are priced by height, not age. Under 120cm = €14; over 120cm = €35 (adult). Check your kids' heights before booking to budget correctly.
🛣️
AP-7 motorway tolls
The drive between Barcelona and Salou uses the AP-7 motorway. Budget ~€8–12 per journey in tolls. Keep coins or a card ready at the toll booth. Total distance is about 110km.
Practical info
🌡️
Weather in late June
Expect 28–33°C in both Barcelona and Salou. Sun from morning to evening. Pack light clothing, factor-50 sunscreen (especially for the kids), hats, and refillable water bottles.
🕘
Spanish meal times
Lunch 2pm–4pm, dinner from 9pm onwards. Restaurants before 8pm are quiet; after 9pm they fill up. Kids menus (menú infantil) are common at family restaurants across Catalonia.

Your Salou apartment
Carrer de la Verge del Pilar is very central — walking distance to beaches and restaurants, and just 2km from PortAventura World. With the rental car you also have easy access to Cambrils.
